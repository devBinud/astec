About NGC

NATIONAL GREEN CORPS (NGC)

National Green Corps (NGC) is a major initiative of Ministry of Environment, Forests and Climate Change (MoEFCC) for creating environmental awareness. It was launched in 2001-02 and aims at building cadres of young children working towards environmental conservation and sustainable development. In Assam, Assam Science Technology and Environment Council has been acting as the State Nodal Agency (SNA) since 2002-2003 involving lakhs of school children and teachers across all 33 districts of Assam. The Council has appointed District Nodal Agencies (DNA) for monitoring district level activities under the programme. Assam currently has more than 8000 School Eco Clubs and 100 College Eco Clubs.

ASTEC

ASSAM SCIENCE TECHNOLOGY & ENVIRONMENT COUNCIL

The Assam Science Technology and Environment Council is representing as State Nodal Agency (SNA) for implementing NGC programmes Assam. The Assam Science Technology & Environment Council (ASTEC) was constituted in 1986-87 as an autonomous Council of the Department of Science Technology & Environment, Govt. of Assam for implementation of some of the major programmes in the sectors of science & technology, remote sensing, energy (non-conventional & renewable sources) and environment as the institutional organization of the Department.

Roles

ROLE OF DIFFERENT STAKEHOLDERS

Role of Eco Clubs

Organize talks on environmental issues in schools, spread environmental awareness at public places, Organize field visits to environmentally important sites. Mobilize action against environmentally unsound practices and help in maintenance of the public places. Demonstrate/promote eco-friendly practices. Build water-harvesting structures in schools, cultivate kitchen gardens, keep vermin compost pits, practise paper recycling, etc. Prepare inventories of polluting sources and forward it to enforcement agencies. Help in planned conservation efforts.

Role of District Nodal Agency

Enrolment of School Eco Clubs in the district including MV, ME, High & HSS. Data collection, compilation, and submission provided by the State Nodal Agency (ASTEC). Inspire all the district's schools and institutions to take part in the aforementioned activities. Regular monitoring and reporting of school Eco club activities to the State Nodal Agency. Each DNA will assist concerned Deputy Commissioner / Inspector of Schools in taking initiatives to constitute a District Implementation and Monitoring Committee (DIMC).

Role of State Nodal Agency

The State Nodal Agency (SNA) for implementing NGC programmes is the Assam Science, Technology, and Environment Council. In Assam, the District Nodal Agencies that ASTEC appoints directly supervise school eco clubs, while ASTEC directly supervises college eco clubs. Conducting different eco-friendly programmes & creating awareness among the public on environmental issues of the world. Disbursement of necessary funds to all concerned Eco Clubs & DNAs for strengthening, coordination & monitoring as per MoEFCC allocation only.
